Jimi Hendrix, playing his signature upside down Fender Stratocaster on stage in 1967.

    The Life of a Legend:

  • 1942 - Born in Seattle, Washington.
  • 1957 - Began to play guitar at the age of 15.
  • 1961 - Enlisted in the US Army and was assigned to the 101st Airbone Division.
  • 1962 - Discharged from the Army after injury during a militar excersize.
  • 1966 - After years as a backup guitarist for the likes of Little Richard and the Isley Brothers, he moves to Englad
  • 1967 - Achieves fame in the states after he set his guitar ablaze at the Monterrey Pop Festival.
  • 1968 - The Jimi Hendrix Exprience records their 3rd studio album, Electric Ladyland.
  • 1969 - Hendrix headlines the Woodstock Festival and performs a unique rendition of the Star Spangled Banner
  • 8/1970 - Hendrix headlines the Isle of Wight Festival
  • 9/1970 - Jimi is found, unresponsive, and dies of barbiturate-related asphyxia.
